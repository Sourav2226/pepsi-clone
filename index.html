Be Young, Have Fun,

Drink Pepsi

Pepsi has come up with various taglines, some of them memorably anthemic for their eras, over the years to connect with the Indian consumer. In 1992, it had “Yahi Hai Right Choice Baby”, which was followed by “Yeh Dil Maange More” in 1998. Prior to “Rise Up, Baby”, its tagline was “Har Ghoont Mein Swag”.
